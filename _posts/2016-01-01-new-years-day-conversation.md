My 6-year-old son: “Dad, is this year a regular year?”

Me: “What do you mean?”

Son: “Is this year a regular year?”

I think for a couple seconds. “Oh. No, this is a leap year.”

Son: “Darnit!”

Me: “Why?”

Son: “Longer until Christmas.”
